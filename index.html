How OnDeck Works

Not another ranking list. A focused answer to who is on deck next and why.

1

Path

We look for players whose organization has a clear reason to push them forward: roster need, level dominance, injury pressure, 40-man timing, or assignment fit.

2

Readiness

Level, age, role, recent form, and trend data help separate a true next-move candidate from a long-range prospect name.

3

Card market

For Top 10 players only, the profile tracks one primary Bowman prospect card so the baseball catalyst and market move can be read together.
